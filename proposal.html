NBA Stats Project Proposal

Problem Description

Nowadays, where technology and advances in computing power thrive, accessing the desired information is easier than ever. However, sometimes overwhelming information might result in confusion or inefficiency in decision making; in modern settings where results need to be delivered quickly, precision, accuracy, and optimization are the utmost critical elements for success.

The NBA statistical data provides insights into a player's guards, forwards, centers, points scored, etc. People utilize this information to make a living by predicting the results and outcome of matches. However, with more than 4,500 players in the NBA, the time consumption for an accurate prediction is extensive.

Although many sites on the web provide the NBA's statistical data, such as ESPN, and sites that offer insights into matches, such as pickswise, they are not sufficient. There is no tool that can efficiently customize games and compare players' statistics in the simulated game.

Proposed Application

The users of this application would be fantasy basketball players and the sports betting community. In addition, scouts can also use our application for some comparison functions.
